Walks
  • Cefn and Bodandreg walk

    A circular 4.6 mile track from The Moorings. With views of the Menai Straits and Snowdonia. Passing cows, sheep, horses, donkeys and llamas.

  • Anglesey coastal path

    A 130 mile foot path that follows much of the island’s coastline.

  • Glan Faenol wildlife and woodland walk

    Explore the walled woodland that is Glan Faenol. Catch a glimpse of a seal in the Menai Strait and see how many bird species you can identify from the hides and platforms.

  • Lon Las Menai

    A 6.5km cycle and walking highway connecting Y Felinheli to Caernarfon.

  • Pyg Track

    Shortest route up Snowdon with the least amount of ascent – but far from being the easiest.

  • Snowdonia Walks

    A great selection of walks within Snowdonia

Attractions
  • Plas Menai

    The National Outdoor Centre for Wales. Activities include; sailing, kayaking, windsurfing, surfing, paddleboarding and much more. Only a 5 minute drive from The Moorings.

  • Zip World

    Home to Velocity 2, the fastest zip line in the world and the longest in Europe.

  • Plas Newydd House and Garden

    Enchanting mansion and gardens, with spectacular views of Snowdonia.

  • Foel Farm Park

    Near Brynsiencyn on Anglesey offering a great family visit to experience the sights, sounds and smells of a real working farm.

  • Rib Ride

    The world’s fastest RIB ride.

  • Anglesey Sea Zoo

    A unique aquarium with over 40 tanks displaying the best of British marine wildlife.

  • Halen Mon

    Anglesey sea salt.

  • Snowdon Mountain Railway

    Since 1896 Snowdon Mountain Railway has been welcoming visitors to Llanberis, the little village at the foot of Snowdon. From Llanberis you can experience the awe inspiring journey by rack and pinion railway to the summit of Snowdon, the highest mountain in Wales and England.
