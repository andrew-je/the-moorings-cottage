Check in time is 5.00pm on your holiday start date (normally Fridays) prior to this time our cleaners will still be in the property. Check out time is no later than 9.00am on the day of departure (normally Friday). The property should be left as you found it on arrival (clean and tidy). For COVID safety reasons we need you to strip the beds that have been used and put the linen in the bags provided and seal them. The towels should all be collected up and sealed in the bags provided.
